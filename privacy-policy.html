Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Corvo Benefits Group LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you contact us or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name and contact information (phone, email, mailing address)
  • Date of birth and Medicare eligibility information
  • ZIP code and geographic location
  • Insurance enrollment preferences and health history summaries
  • Prescription medication information (for plan comparison purposes only)

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Browser type and operating system
  • IP address and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website URLs

We do NOT collect: Full Social Security numbers, complete Medicare numbers, or banking information through this website. If you must share sensitive information, we recommend calling us directly rather than using online forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Medicare plan consultation and enrollment services
  • Contact you to discuss your Medicare options and next steps
  • Compare available plans based on your health and coverage needs
  • Comply with CMS (Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services) regulations and Agent & Broker rules
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send informational emails and educational content (only if you opt-in)
  • Maintain records for agent licensing and compliance purposes

3. Health Information & HIPAA

When you share health information with us (such as medications or doctor names for plan comparison), we handle this information with the same privacy protections as healthcare providers. To the extent that we receive, create, or maintain protected health information (PHI), we comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) Privacy and Security Rules.

Your health information is only used to help us recommend appropriate Medicare plans and is never shared with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • As required by law or CMS regulations
  • With the insurance carriers whose plans we are comparing for you

4. Information Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

We may share your information with:

  • Insurance Carriers: When necessary to process Medicare plan enrollment or complete your request
  • CMS: As required by law for agent and broker compliance reporting
  • Service Providers: Limited third parties who assist with website hosting, email delivery, and CRM services (all bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request

5. Data Security

We maintain appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption for data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You acknowledge the inherent risks of transmitting information over the Internet.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • File a complaint with applicable privacy authorities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@corvobenefits.com or call (XXX) XXX-XXXX.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve site functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Because there is no industry standard for recognizing this signal, our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track requests. However, you can manage cookie and tracking preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

10. California & Other State Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). If you are a resident of another state with specific privacy laws, similar rights may apply. Please contact us for more information about your specific privacy rights.
